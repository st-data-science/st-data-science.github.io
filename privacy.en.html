Privacy Policy

Social Data Science Lab (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") shall promote the protection of personal information by establishing a system for the protection of personal information, recognizing the importance of the protection of personal information, and making thorough efforts to do so.

Personal Information Management

We will maintain accurate and up-to-date personal information, and take necessary measures such as the maintenance of security systems to prevent unauthorized access, loss, damage, falsification, and leakage of personal information, as well as implement safety measures to strictly control personal information.

Purpose of Use of Personal Information

We will use your personal information to contact you, to guide you through our work, and to reply to your questions by email or by sending you materials.

Prohibition of Disclosure and Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The Company will manage personal information provided by the customer in an appropriate manner and will not disclose personal information to a third party except in the following cases. 1) When the customer agrees to disclose personal information 2) When the Company needs to disclose personal information to a subcontractor in order to provide the service the customer wishes to receive. 3) When it is necessary to disclose the information in accordance with laws and regulations.

Personal Information Security Measures

The Company takes all possible security measures to ensure the accuracy and safety of personal information.

Inquiry of the Person in Question

When a customer requests access to, correction of, or deletion of his or her personal information, we will respond to the customer's request after confirming his or her identity.

Compliance and Review of Laws, Regulations and Standards

The Company shall comply with the laws and regulations of Japan and other standards applicable to personal information held by the Company, and shall review and improve this policy from time to time.
