About Me

Suguru Tanaka is a data scientist and an individual business owner of Social Data Science Lab since 2019.
He completed MSc Data Science program at the London School of Economics and advised by Milan Vojnovic, Chair of Data Science.
Before that, he graduated as a Salutatorian from Tokyo Institue of Technology, the most prestigious engineering university in Japan.

Suguru won the Best Bachelor's Thesis Award from the Department of Social Engineering from Tokyo Institue of Technology.
He is an authorized statistician accredited by the Royal Statistical Society, one of the most important organization for statisticians and data scientists in the world.
